How to play

Welcome to The Elements Game!
You are given the option to choose between four elements:
Fire, Water, Air and Ice.
The computer will randomly pick one of the four elements. Depending on what you and the computer have picked a winner is chosen based on the following rules:

If your choice and the computer's one match, it is a draw and you both receive a point.

For the Wins:
Fire wins over Ice. Water wins over Fire. Fire wins over Air. Ice wins over Water.

For the loses:
Fire loses to Water. Air loses to Fire. Water loses to Ice. Ice loses to Fire.

Specific draw situations:
It is also a draw when:
Water ends up against Air. Air ends up against Ice. Air ends up against Water. Ice ends up against Air.
Whoever first receives 10 points wins the game!
If you and the computer receive 10 points at the same time it's a draw!
